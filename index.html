Mission

We think that by giving children with disabilities employment possibilities, we may contribute to the removal of stigma and the advancement of a more inclusive society. We want to demonstrate that children with disabilities have the same talents, skills, and goals as other children, and that, given the correct opportunity and assistance, they can succeed in their careers and contribute significantly to society.

Vision

No matter what their abilities, we at Aashiyan think that everyone deserves the opportunity to work and make a contribution to society. We are committed to giving special adults career chances as they frequently struggle to find jobs owing to their disability.We collaborate closely with companies and organisations to design jobs that are specific to the skills and abilities of special adults.
